MEMORIES....

The DGB

Memories of friendship are treasures that we cherish forever. And for us, the gang called DGB holds the most precious memories of our lives. Named after Vasim's grandfather's favorite word, this gang comprises of ten members - Sami, Skymone, Maman, Ashu, Brucelee, Pulukka, Chunni, Remeshan, Vasim, and Oman.


Our friendship began on November 17, 2021, when we met in the computer batch in 11th class. From that day on, we shared endless laughter, countless memories, and infinite joy. And as our batch comes to an end on March 30, 2023, we know that our friendship will last a lifetime.


But there was one particular moment that stands out from all others. After the exams in 11th class on June 30, 2022, a heavy rainstorm hit our city. We were at the RP Mall and decided to head to GMax in the rain. As we ran through the streets, drenched to the bone, people looked at us with curiosity. But we didn't care. For us, the only thing that mattered was being together. We ended up in an empty theater, watching "Minions: The Rise of Guru," and it was the best moment of our lives. The laughter, the jokes, and the memories we made that day will stay with us forever.


As our class comes to an end, we know that our friendship will live on. The memories we created together will remain etched in our hearts forever. We may go our separate ways, but we will always have each other. For DGB, it's not just a gang, it's a bond that we will cherish until the end of time.
